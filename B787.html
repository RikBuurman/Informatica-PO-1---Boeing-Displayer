The 787-8 can hold 242 passengers in a three-class configuration and it can hold 264 passengers in a two-class configuration. There have been 374 planes built in total and there are still 48 that are being built at this moment. It has a length of 56,7 m. And a maximum weight of 228.000 kg, it can also hold a maximum of 126.920 L of kerosine and with this amount of fuel, it can fly a maximum of 15.200 km. It has a height of 17 m and a wingspan of 60 m. The first flight was on 15 December 2009. The maximum starting weight is 250.836 kg and there have been a total of 1441 planes built.
