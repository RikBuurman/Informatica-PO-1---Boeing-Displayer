The 777-200 can hold 305 passengers in a three-class configuration. The first flight was on June 12, 1994. There have been made 88 airplanes for a total of 9 diffrent airlines. It has a maximum flight range of 9.700 km and it can go to a speed of 950 km/h. The 777-200 is not in production anymore. And it has a maximum departure weight of 247.000 kg.
