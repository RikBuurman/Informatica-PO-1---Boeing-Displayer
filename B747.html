The 747-400 seats 416 passengers in a three-class or 524 passengers in a two-class lauout. The 737-800 was launched on April 29. 1988. The 747-400 burns 3.302 US gallons (12.500 L) of jet fuel per hour. It has a wingspan of 65,90 m and is 19,06 m high it weighs 178.756 kg without anything in it and it has a maximum of 396.890 kg at takeoff. Its first flight was on the 29th of April 1988. It can reach a max velocity of 913 km/h and can fly 13,350 km.
