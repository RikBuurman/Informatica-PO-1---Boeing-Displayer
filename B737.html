The 737-800 seats 162 passengers in a two-class layout or 189 passengers in a one-class layout. The 737−800 was launched on September 5, 1994. The 737-800 burns 850 US gallons (3,200 L) of jet fuel per hour, which is equal to around 4.88 US gal (18.5 L) per seat per hour. As of May 2019, Boeing had delivered 4,979 737-800s, which mostley are in the hands of Southwest Airlines. It has two engines. It has a wingspan of 28,88 m. The biggest airline that has the most 737-800 is Southwest Airlines from America and the seccond biggest is Ryanair. The 737-800 is part of the next-generation fleet of the Boeing 737’s.
